History

Rafting Momentum was born in the heart of Bryson, Quebec, surrounded by the stunning natural beauty of Canada’s wilderness. Founded by a group of passionate adventurers, the company started with a simple goal: to share the thrill of whitewater rafting with others.

Over the years, Rafting Momentum has become a go-to destination for those seeking adventure, offering expert-guided trips that showcase the power of the rapids and the tranquility of the landscape. Whether you’re a seasoned rafter or a first-timer, their mission remains the same—to deliver an unforgettable experience on the water.
